Overview

This audience testing framework provides a structured approach to testing different audience segments across TikTok, Snapchat, and Instagram platforms. The framework includes interest-based targeting, behavioral targeting, lookalike audiences, and retargeting strategies.

Campaign Name Format: SIRFRANCIS_AUDTEST_[PLATFORM]_[DATE]
Interest-Based Targeting

Target audiences based on their demonstrated interests and affinities.

Audience Set Target Group Interests/Behaviors Budget/Day Success Metrics
A1_LUXURY Luxury Shoppers Luxury brands, premium shopping, high-end retail 150 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
A2_FRAGRANCE Fragrance Enthusiasts Perfumes, colognes, essential oils, aromatherapy 150 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
A3_GIFTING Gift Purchasers Gift shopping, special occasions, celebrations 150 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
Implementation Notes:
  • Test each interest group individually before combining
  • Monitor frequency and audience fatigue
  • Consider platform-specific interest targeting options
Behavioral Targeting

Target audiences based on their online behaviors and actions.

Audience Set Target Group Behaviors Budget/Day Success Metrics
B1_SHOPPERS Active Shoppers Online purchases (30 days), add to cart activities 200 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
B2_ENGAGEMENT Content Engagers Video viewers, ad engagers, social media engagers 200 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
Implementation Notes:
  • Behavioral targeting tends to have higher intent than interest targeting
  • Ensure proper pixel implementation for accurate behavioral tracking
  • Consider recency of behaviors when building audiences
Lookalike Audiences

Build audiences that share characteristics with your existing high-value customers.

Important: Implement after gathering sufficient data
Audience Set Source Audience Expansion Budget/Day Success Metrics
L1_PURCHASERS Past Purchasers 1% LAL 250 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
L2_HIGHVALUE High-Value Customers 1% LAL 250 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
L3_ENGAGEMENT Website Visitors 2% LAL 200 SAR CAC < 35 SAR
Source Audience Requirements
  • Minimum 1,000 users for effective LAL creation
  • Quality over quantity for source audiences
  • Recent data (last 90-180 days) performs best
Platform Considerations
  • TikTok: 1% LAL ~200K users in Saudi
  • Snapchat: Smaller LAL sizes but higher precision
  • Instagram: Most mature LAL technology
Retargeting

Re-engage users who have previously interacted with your brand or website.

Audience Set Source Window Budget/Day Success Metrics
R1_CART Cart Abandoners 7 Days 100 SAR ROAS > 8x
R2_PRODUCT Product Page Viewers 14 Days 100 SAR ROAS > 6x
R3_VISITORS All Site Visitors 30 Days 100 SAR ROAS > 5x
Retargeting Best Practices:
Timing
  • Recency matters - prioritize recent visitors
  • Test different window periods
  • Consider frequency capping
Messaging
  • Use specific messaging based on intent
  • Consider special offers for cart abandoners
  • Address common objections
Technical Setup
  • Ensure proper pixel implementation
  • Set up event-specific audiences
  • Apply exclusions for purchasers
Regional Considerations

Tailor your targeting approach based on regional differences within Saudi Arabia.

Region Targeting Approach Notes
Riyadh High Priority Largest market, cultural connection
Jeddah Urban Luxury Cosmopolitan audience
Eastern Province Oil Industry Higher income demographics
Secondary Cities Value Proposition Focus on exclusivity
Gender Strategy

Test both dedicated campaigns and combined approaches:

  • Male-specific campaigns (masculine fragrances)
  • Female-specific campaigns (feminine fragrances)
  • Unisex campaigns (with segmentation at ad level)
Testing Rules
  • Minimum test duration 7 days
  • Minimum impressions per audience 1,000
  • Minimum clicks for statistical significance 100
Additional Guidelines
  • Use clear naming convention for accurate tracking
  • Utilize control audiences for valid comparison
  • Only test one variable at a time
  • Document all test results thoroughly
  • Apply learnings to future campaigns
